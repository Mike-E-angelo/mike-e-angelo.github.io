Lead / words / 2012 / Let

February 4, 2012

How do I live up to this?
	Looking to the side stage.
	My Stage.
		You caught it, you bought it.
			I’m flipping caps here.
			I don’t think I can dance this stepfloor.
				… and then…
				“I’m sort of interested in honesty.”
				Ya, what is honesty, anyways?
					Honesty is reaching for the wrong bottle.
					And knowing it.
					Honesty is predicting the future.
					Then facing it.
					Honesty is consistency.
				Didn’t you say that consistency is key?
					That’s a great question.
					My sentence is better.
				There are those that say…
					Every word.  Every letter.
					An encapsulation of thought.
					Something you should have fought for.
				Oh, influence.
						Is just a concept.
					Anything else?
						I’m sure I’ll find the words for it.
