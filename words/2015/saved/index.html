Steel / words / 2015 / Saved

July 1, 2015

Where hare you?
Are you in the blue.

Are you here in the stead.
Are you here in my head.

I feel the taste.

Electric paste.
The moment between moments.

A calm buzz.
A careening presumption.

A black white hum.
Subtle space.

Can you feel me?

It’s a special space.
A 12 step process.

Five planting.
7 climbing.

Somewhere it is there.
I wear it briefly.

A sudden god.
