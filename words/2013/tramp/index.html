Lead / words / 2013 / Tramp

November 2, 2013

A god,
	Broken into billions of pieces.
	A piece is you.

		To gather the pieces is… a clue.

			Are you a piece of me?
	Am I a piece of you?
		How do I see this piece of me?
			Where?
	Shattered.
		Assembled whole.
				Ocean images.

						You must show something for your efforts.
						Something.
			There are trysts in these parts.
	There are trusts, too.
					I have the karma divide.
					She is not very nice.

	Q				I might be lying.

							Would you pick up and move?
							Would I fantasize about you?

							No.  And yes, I do.
						How do I lose you?
						How do I lose you?
