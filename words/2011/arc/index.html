Lead / words / 2011 / Arc

July 5, 2011

Step Four.
	Nothing more.
		Nothing less.
		Universe can be explained to you.
		To me.
		Your answer is your key.
		It’s simple.
		Masterpiece.
		A matter of relativity.
		You hold in your hands this matter.
		It’s a silver platter.
			Subject to respect
			Circumspection
			Restoration.
		Your spirit holds
			The inseminated images
				Of cinema.
				Unfolding your mind
				In a myriad of different fathoms.
			A bird’s beak is a delicate name, madam.
			In this Scorpio’s nest, a Brother brings his best.
		Certainly not on your playground.
		But the ground rules are set.
		Bring on the next round, child.
			We will see your twisted net.
