Lead / words / 2011 / Sand

November 25, 2011

A cool effervescence
	Of some sort.
Don’t do what you do.
		Do what you do.
To me,
	There are two forces in life,
	There’s the reptile
		And there’s the Space Angel
			===
		The art of these two warring fractions
		Is you.
What does it mean
	To be free?
	A free man.
		Last time I checked.
		There are lots of definitions of free.
		We’re not gonna get there, are we?
	Someone over here says…
		Sin virtue and vice.
		All very nice.
		Until you stick 
			A book In 
			A shaded back pocket
			Partnered associate.
Glass eyed?
