Gold / prayers / 2018 / Grain

June 2, 2018

Cherished Creator,
May You create in Us

     The World that is Yours
     Within Us

We bow now with wow
A bow wow for now.

In Your breathing essence
The stars stir with wonder

In Your high esteem
We beam a stately seam

May Your courtly desires
Praise that which is Noble and Pure

A high account of Heaven
Blessed be our ascent

Within Your blessed bough
Of might and brow

May we pass through that which is
Destined for The Bright

Derive that which breeds perfection
Deliver us unto Your predilection

May my friends be hardened
Warriors of Your Light

Tallied and stoned towards righteousness
To protect the weak, preserve the innocent

Such pure presence and power!
Such pristine, abiding towers!

Wonder and malfeasance
One and Other

Truly to be born of You
And to be Us

Traveling a thousand Suns
Borne by a thousand Nations

Unified and harnessed
Consecrating star discs

Protect the poor and wasteful
Assigning the protectorate in full

Safety and protection in our daily travels
Whispered intercessions and marvels

Awash us with visions and fortitude
In abundance of health, forthright, and multitude
