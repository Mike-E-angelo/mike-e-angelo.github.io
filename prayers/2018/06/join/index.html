Gold / prayers / 2018 / Join

June 14, 2018

May my timing be appropriate and crisp
According to Your will

May Your animals be swift and decisive
Mightily dodging Sofia’s tread

May these furry fellas miss us
May my braking be prudent and sure

May their appropriate signs be taken
And understood and received accordingly

May these signs never result in any death, injury
Or damage

May I also be kindly reminded to further
Not to partake of the death of animals in my
Daily bread

May You guide us on our daily journeys
May they include Your great graces and timing

May I learn to enjoy the rabbit, the squirrel, the deer
The robin, and the blue jay too

May their signs be known to me
So I know what to do

For You
