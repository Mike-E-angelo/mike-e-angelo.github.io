Gold / prayers / 2018 / Case

June 20, 2018

May Your stories ring clear and
	May I know which one to use
		At which time
		
May those who wish to support us
	Be gainfully rewarded
May their contributions be properly
	Bestowed and invested
		In manifold
May they also be known in 
	Full regard and call
May they know their calling here
	By these words
		In past, present, and future

May those who wish to contribute
	Continue to have patience
May I know them by their words
	And subsequent actions 

May Your visions continue to
	Ring clear and with Heart
May I accept and knead them within

May You work with me in esteem and honor

May Your Court be 
	filled with praise
		to the end of days
